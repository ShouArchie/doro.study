Course Description

ECE 405C:

The course will teach basic elements to create quantum circuits in quantum computers: qubits, single-qubit gates, two-qubit gates, quantum operators, and measurements. A quantum programming language (one of three: Qiskit, q#, Pennylane) is used to implement representative quantum circuits in quantum simulators or real quantum computers through a cloud service such as IBM Q experience or Microsoft Azure or Xanadu.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course students should be able to:
Learn the basic elements of quantum circuits
Learn the fundamentals of representative quantum algorithms
Learn how to program quantum circuits and quantum algorithms via quantum computers or quantum cloud services
Learn how to analyze the results from quantum computing and programming
