Course Description

COMM 433:

The course is designed to provide the student with broad overview of the Canadian income tax system as it relates to corporations and individuals.

View requirements for COMM 433

COURSE DESCRIPTION AND OBJECTIVES:

The objectives of this course are for students to become more familiar with the way Canadian corporations are taxed. Topics covered include a brief history of taxation in Canada, the structure of Canadian tax legislation, administrative and enforcement details, the calculation of income for tax purposes and tax payable, as well as an introduction to tax planning opportunities for Canadian corporations.

TAX ASSIGNMENT

Details will be provided in a separate handout but the objective of this assignment is to integrate your knowledge of taxationwith written and verbal communication skills. Students must select a topic from a list provided, write a report on the case, and present the case in class.
