Course Description

MATH 148:

MATH148 is an advanced-level version of MATH138.

View requirements for MATH 148

Riemann integration, fundamental theorem of calculus, techniques of integration, areas and volumes, series of real numbers, convergence tests for series, absolute/conditional convergence and rearrangement theorems, power series, term-by-term differentiation/integration of power series, Taylor series, differential equations. 

Learning Outcomes

In this course you will:
1. Rigorously study single-variable, Riemann integration.
2. Apply integration to solve area and volume problems.
3. Thoroughly investigate the theory of series of real numbers.
4. Carefully study the theory of series of functions, with particular emphasis on power series and Taylor series.
5. Be introduced to some theory of differential equations.
