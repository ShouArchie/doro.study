Course Description

MATH 148:

MATH148 is an advanced-level version of MATH138.

View requirements for MATH 148

Learning Outcomes

In this course, you will:
Explore the theory of the Riemann integral
Learn how to calculate a variety of elementary integrals and how to use integration to solve practical problems
Explore infinite series of real numbers and real-valued functions
Learn how to test an infinite series for convergence
Explore the Taylor series expansion of a real-valued function
Be introduced to the theory of ordinary differential equations

Tentative Course Schedule

The Riemann integral (~6 weeks)
Series (~5 weeks)
Differential equations (~1 week)

Administrative Policy

Group Work Policy: You are encouraged to discuss assignments problems with your classmates. The discussion should be at a high level and you should not take notes during such discussions. Do not share or copy complete solutions. All submitted work must be written up individually. Collaborators and any other outside sources of help must be properly acknowledged.

Late Assignment Policy: -20% per hour late. No extensions.

Missed Assignment Policy: With instructor-approved excuse (e.g. documented illness), weight will be shifted to the other assignments. No make-up assignments or extensions.

Missed Test Policy: With instructor-approved excuse (e.g. documented illness), weight will be shifted to final exam. No make-up tests.
