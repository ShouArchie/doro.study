Instructor & TA (Teaching Assistant) Information

 

Course Description

SYDE 182:

Kinematics of particles, rectilinear and curvilinear motion. Kinetics of particles, application to space mechanics. Energy and momentum methods. Systems of particles. Kinematics and kinetics of rigid bodies; planar motion.

View requirements for SYDE 182

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course students should be able to:
Understand Fundamental Principles of Dynamics
Analyze Kinematics of Particles and Rigid Bodies
Apply Kinetics to Solve Problems
Model Real-World Dynamic Systems
Investigate Work-Energy and Impulse-Momentum Principles
Understand the Dynamics of Rotational Motion
