Course Description

ME 340:

The principles of manufacturing unit processes including casting, forming, machining and joining. Interactions between design, materials (metals, polymers, ceramics) and processes. Advantages and limitations, relative cost, and production rates of competitive processes.

View requirements for ME 340

At the end of the class students will learn fundamentals of traditional manufacturing such as casting, forming and chips removal (machining) and advanced and contemporary manufacturing techniques such as nano/micro-machining.
