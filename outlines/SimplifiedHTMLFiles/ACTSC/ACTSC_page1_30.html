Course Description

ACTSC 624:

Counting processes; Markov processes and Kolmogorov equations; Brownian motion and geometric Brownian motion; Ito's lemma Monte Carlo simulation.

View requirements for ACTSC 624

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course students should be able to:
Introduce basic probability theory
Introduce discrete-time and continuous-time Markov chains
Introduce martingales, Brownian motion, and basic stochastic calculus

Tentative Course Schedule

1. Introduction to probability (2 weeks)

2. Discrete-Time Markov Chains (2.5 weeks)

3. Continuous-Time Markov Chains (2.5 weeks)

4. Martingales (2.5 weeks)

5. Brownian Motion and Stochastic Calculus (2.5 weeks)
