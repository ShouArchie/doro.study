Course Description

PHYS 335:

Overview of condensed matter ordered and disordered systems. Thermodynamic origin of order and phase transitions. Waves. Properties of the solid state. Crystals and fractals. Overview of Fourier Series. Reciprocal lattice. Diffraction. Classical elastic theory of the crystalline state. Electrons in a periodic potential, Band structure and Fermi surface.

View requirements for PHYS 335

• Crystal structure. Bravais lattices, classification of Bravais lattices by point group symmetries.

• Reciprocal lattice, Fourier analysis and X-ray diﬀraction.

• Crystal binding and elasticity.

• Theory of lattice vibrations. Phonons.

• Free electron theory of metals.

• Band theory. Metals and Insulators.

• Basics of the theory of transport, Boltzmann equation.

• Basics of semiconductor physics, semiconductor devices.
